\chapter{Results}
\section{Overview}
This chapter presents the validation process and results of the processes outlined Chapter 
\ref{sec:methodology}. The results are organised by processing operation. Each section
discusses the correctness and computational performance of an algorithm, as well as the results of 
the ablation studies. The results are presented in the form of tables, figures, and plots, and are 
accompanied by a discussion of their implications
for the design of the radar processing pipeline. The results are also compared to the state of the art 
in the literature, and are used to derive design recommendations for software-defined radar processing 
pipelines under SWaP constraints.
