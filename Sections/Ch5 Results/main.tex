\chapter{Results}
\section{Overview}
This chapter presents the validation process and results of the processes outlined in Chapter 
\ref{sec:methodology}. The results are organised by operation. Each section
discusses the correctness and computational performance of an algorithm, as well as the results of 
the ablation studies. The results are presented as plots, and are 
accompanied by a discussion of their implications for the design of the radar processing pipeline
in the following chapter.
