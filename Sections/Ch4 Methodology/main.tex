\section{Overview}
\begin{itemize}
    \item The methodology of this thesis involves designing and implementing parts of a software-defined radar processing pipeline for a small monopulse radar system, and evaluating its performance in terms of both computational efficiency and tracking accuracy.
    \item The first sections in this define the pipeline, its applications and their constituent operations. The questions and solution space for each operation is outlined, and the questions mirror the gaps identified in the literature review.
    \item Then the section describes the datasets that will be used for testing, which include both synthetic data and real radar data recorded from our AWR2243 radar system.    
    \item Lastly the section outlines the difference techniques used to evaluate the performance of the software pipeline, the metrics we will report, and the tools we will use for performance analysis.
    \item The evaluation procedure for each operation in our pipeline is outlined in Sections \ref{sec:processing_app}, and \ref{sec:tracking_app}. \item The experiments are designed to fill the identified gaps in the literature.   
\end{itemize}
